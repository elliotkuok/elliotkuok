About Me

I spent a considerable amount of time post grad trying to get a company to sponsor my visa for the U.S.. After looking at a number of digital marketing agencies, I grew tired of seeing the same narrative of companies boasting about their "diversity" only to see that people in positions of leadership were either white or male (in almost all cases, leaders were both). So I said f*** it. I'm going to sponsor my own visa by creating a company where someone like me, a trans, queer, Asian immigrant, doesn't feel like my presence is just the product of trending capitalistic diversity.

And that's what I'm up to now. Working to build a company that aims to empower those from marginalized communities who want to work in the web development and digital marketing industry.
